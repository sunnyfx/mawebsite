My name is Sunny and this is my company Sunnyfx

Ever since I was little, I've been attracted to the spookier and more gorey side of makeup and special effects. Halloween has always been my inspiration for the monsters I create and the photo shoots I create. I love doing very detailed work and really paying attention to the little details that the clients want.

I also love being new things to the industry like no techniques and different styles. I love exploring in my work and really showing my vision in my work and how it makes my viewers feel when they see it.
